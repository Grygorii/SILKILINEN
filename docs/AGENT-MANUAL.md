# SILKILINEN — How to run your AI marketing team

A practical guide for the founder. See `docs/agent-garden.svg` for the visual map.

## The one rule
**The agents draft, you decide.** Nothing public (article, post, email, SEO change) goes live on its own — it's created as a *draft* you approve with one click. Let them run freely; you stay in control.

## Step 1 — Set your AI Star (do this first)
**Admin → Growth Engine → AI Star → Edit.** Pick the goal (e.g. *8 orders/month by Nov 2026*). Everything steers toward it.

## Step 2 — The chain (who decides what)
Layered by horizon — each serves the one above, so they never contradict:

| Level | Who | Horizon | You touch it… |
|---|---|---|---|
| 🎼 **Da Vinci** | the composer | **quarter** (90 days) | rarely — a reset/big push |
| 🧠 **Chief of Staff** | the brain | **this week** | weekly (read it) |
| 🎯 **Marketing Coordinator** | the lead | **a campaign/goal** | when you want to push something |
| ⚙️ **Growth Engine** | the specialists | **continuous** | to approve their drafts |

Use the level that matches your decision — the lower ones inherit from it.

## Step 3 — Weekly rhythm (~10 min)
1. **Admin → Growth Engine** → read the **Chief of Staff brief** (the week's honest state + 3-4 moves; already aligned to the quarter).
2. Scroll **The Pulse** → **approve or reject** the queued drafts.
3. Glance at **Admin → Journeys** → the funnel + what visitors searched/clicked (your reality check).

## Step 4 — Push something specific
**Admin → Marketing Coordinator** → type a goal (*"Push sleep dresses for summer"*) → **Coordinate**. You get one plan: the insight, the specialists it delegates to, and tasks with "open →" links. Tick them off.

## Step 5 — Quarterly / big reset
**Admin → Growth Engine → unleash Da Vinci** → a bold 90-day vision the weekly briefs then serve.

## The specialists
| Agent | Does | Output lands in |
|---|---|---|
| Demand Scout | rising searches/trends | Growth pulse |
| Competitor Scout | the rival field's positioning | Growth pulse |
| Storefront Scout | rivals' UX/merchandising ideas | Growth pulse |
| Hermes (SEO) | what you rank for + how to win clicks | SEO → Recommendations |
| Content Writer | journal article drafts for real queries | Journal (drafts) |
| Social Drafter | social post drafts | Social (drafts) |
| Newsletter Drafter | email drafts for new pieces | Marketing → Campaigns |
| Eureka / Prometheus / Maui | product ideas / clarity fixes / delight | Growth pulse |
| Watchdog | flags stock/Merchant/review problems | Growth pulse (alerts) |
| Logic + Reasoning Clerks | **verify** everything above | the brief's "Clerks' verdict" |

## Other tools
- **SEO tab** — Hermes recommendations, health, **Rebuild SEO** (one-click fixes), **↻ Resubmit all to IndexNow**.
- **Journal → ✦ Write with AI** — a full "masterpiece" article grounded in all agent data.
- **Site Audit** (Settings → Advanced) — re-checks SEO hygiene (the regression guard) and reports exact problem URLs.
- **Ask AI** — chat the Analyst for a one-off question about your numbers.

## Guardrails (built in)
- Every claim is **fact-checked by the clerks** before you act.
- The team is forbidden from **discounting / price-competing** — quiet luxury, always.
- Only **first-party data** feeds the brain (your own clickstream), so advice is grounded in *your* shoppers.

**Golden path:** Set the AI Star → read the weekly brief → approve drafts → use the Coordinator for a specific push → unleash Da Vinci each quarter.
